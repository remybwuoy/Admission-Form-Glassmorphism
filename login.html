TIPS AND GUIDELINES


Step 1:

Walk into and Banking Hall and pay for your e-Voucher.

Step 2:

Proceed to read the instructions on the Admission Form within the envelope

Step 3:

Make sure you have attained all the necessary perequisites stated within the Voucher before proceeding to begin with the online admission

Step 4:

Fill your admission forms online by providing all the necessary information required and then proceed to complete all stages of filling the other pages of the form in the same manner as you did to the first page by clicking on the button labelled Next.

Step 5:

Finalize your application by clicking the Submit button to submit your form to us after going through at least two times to confirm whether all information provided are valid and true.
